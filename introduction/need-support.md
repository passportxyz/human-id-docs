# Need Support?

If you need support when verifying your identity using Human ID, please follow the steps below:

1. Review or search the contents of this site, especially our [Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)](../for-users/faqs.md).
2. _**Click on the support icon in the bottom right corner of this page**_ — or in the Human ID app — to get in contact with a support agent.
